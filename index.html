Two Sources

Two Places Funds May Appear

Some funds appear in the City and County of Honolulu escheated-check records. These are often older checks issued by the City and County of Honolulu that were not cashed or recovered. Other funds may appear in the State of Hawaii Unclaimed Property database, which includes additional categories of abandoned or dormant property. We help review both sources when appropriate.

🏙️

Honolulu Escheated Checks

Publicly listed checks from the City and County of Honolulu Department of Budget and Fiscal Services. Searchable by payee name, check amount, or check number. This is our primary lead source.

State Unclaimed Property

Separate database maintained by the State of Hawaii for abandoned or dormant property. Includes bank accounts, insurance proceeds, utility deposits, and more. We check this as an added bundled service.
