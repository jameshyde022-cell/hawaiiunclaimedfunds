What We Do

Our Primary Service: Honolulu Escheated Check Recovery

We specialize in reviewing the City and County of Honolulu Department of Budget and Fiscal Services escheated-check database, identifying payees owed checks over $1,000, tracing and contacting them, and helping them through the recovery process. This is our primary lead source and our core service.

As a bundled addition, we also check the State of Hawaii Unclaimed Property database for additional funds that may exist separately. A person or business may appear in one database, both, or neither.

Addressing the Obvious Question

"Why Would I Pay a Fee When I Can File for Free?"

This is exactly the right question to ask, and we respect you for asking it. The honest answer: for some people, you shouldn't hire us.

👍

When to File Yourself

  • Your claim is straightforward — matching name, current address, active ID
  • The amount is small (a few hundred dollars)
  • You have time, patience, and organizational skills
  • No estate, probate, or multiple-claimant situations
  • You're comfortable with government paperwork
See the Free Verification Guide
🤝

When to Hire Us

  • Complex estate claim (deceased relative's funds)
  • Business or corporate claim requiring EIN verification
  • Multiple claimants or heir disputes
  • Large amounts where getting it right really matters
  • Previous claim was denied or delayed
  • You simply prefer someone else to handle it

Why Clients Choose Us

8 Reasons People Hire a Professional

When people choose to hire us for Honolulu escheated-check recovery or State unclaimed property recovery, these are the most common reasons.

📋

Documentation Expertise

We know exactly what's required for each claim type — individual, estate, business — and how to present it correctly. The wrong document or a missing detail can delay or deny a claim by months.

⏱️

Time & Convenience

The claim process can take dozens of hours: researching requirements, gathering documents, completing forms, following up. Our clients pay our fee to get that time back. For many, that's an excellent trade.

👨‍👩‍👧

Estate & Heir Claims

Claiming funds on behalf of a deceased person is significantly more complex. It requires legal documentation proving you are an authorized heir or representative. This applies to both Honolulu escheated checks and State unclaimed property.

🏢

Business Claims

If your business has Honolulu escheated checks or State unclaimed property, you need to prove authorized signatory status, provide EIN documentation, and potentially corporate resolutions. We handle business claims regularly.

🔄

Persistence & Follow-Up

Without professional follow-up, claims can stall without explanation. We proactively monitor status, respond to inquiries immediately, and escalate when appropriate.

🔍

Comprehensive Search

We search both the Honolulu escheated-check database and the State of Hawaii Unclaimed Property database, including alternative name spellings, former addresses, maiden names, and related business entities. Clients frequently discover additional funds they didn't know about.

❌

Prior Denial or Delay

If your previous claim was denied or has been stalled, we can review what happened, identify the issue, and resubmit with the correct documentation.

😌

Peace of Mind

Sometimes the value is simply knowing a professional is handling something important. If you have a large claim or are going through a difficult life event (estate, divorce, medical issues), not having to manage this paperwork is genuinely valuable.
