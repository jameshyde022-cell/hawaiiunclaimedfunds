Addressing the Obvious Question

"Why Would I Pay 20% When I Can File for Free?"

This is exactly the right question to ask, and we respect you for asking it. The honest answer: for some people, you shouldn't hire us.

👍

When to File Yourself

  • Your claim is straightforward — matching name, current address, active ID
  • The amount is small (a few hundred dollars)
  • You have time, patience, and organizational skills
  • No estate, probate, or multiple-claimant situations
  • You're comfortable with government paperwork
See the Free How-To Guide
🤝

When to Hire Us

  • Complex estate claim (deceased relative's funds)
  • Business or corporate claim requiring EIN verification
  • Multiple claimants or heir disputes
  • Large amounts where getting it right really matters
  • Previous claim was denied or delayed
  • You simply prefer someone else to handle it
Get Free Consultation
Our bottom line: If your claim is simple and you're willing to put in the time, go do it yourself at dollars.ehawaii.gov. We'll still be here if you hit a wall.

Why Clients Choose Us

8 Reasons People Hire a Professional

When people choose to hire us, these are the most common reasons. Every one of them is legitimate.

📋

Documentation Expertise

The state has specific, exacting requirements for documentation. We know exactly what's required for each claim type — individual, estate, business — and how to present it. The wrong document or a missing detail can delay or deny a claim by months.

⏱️

Time & Convenience

The claim process can take dozens of hours: researching requirements, gathering documents, completing forms, following up with the state. Our clients pay 20% to get that time back. For many, that's an excellent trade.

👨‍👩‍👧

Estate & Heir Claims

Claiming funds on behalf of a deceased person is significantly more complex. It requires legal documentation proving you are an authorized heir or representative. We handle these frequently and know the exact process the state requires.

🏢

Business Claims

If your business has unclaimed funds, you need to prove authorized signatory status, provide EIN documentation, and potentially corporate resolutions. Mistakes here can result in outright denial. We handle business claims regularly.

🔄

Persistence & Follow-Up

The state processes thousands of claims. Without professional follow-up, claims can stall without explanation. We proactively monitor status, respond to state inquiries immediately, and escalate when appropriate.

🔍

Comprehensive Search

We search beyond what's obvious — including alternative name spellings, former addresses, maiden names, related business entities, and accounts you may have forgotten. Clients frequently discover additional funds they didn't know about.

❌

Prior Denial or Delay

If your previous claim was denied or has been stalled for months without resolution, we can review what happened, identify the issue, and resubmit with the correct documentation.

😌

Peace of Mind

Sometimes the value is simply knowing a professional is handling something important. If you have a large claim or are going through a difficult life event (estate, divorce, medical issues), not having to manage this paperwork is genuinely valuable.
