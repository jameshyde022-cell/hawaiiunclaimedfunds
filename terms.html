1. Services Description

1.1 Nature of Services

Hawaii Unclaimed Funds Recovery is a finder service that assists individuals and businesses in identifying and recovering unclaimed funds from two public sources:

  • Honolulu escheated-check records — the City and County of Honolulu Department of Budget and Fiscal Services maintains a database of escheated checks issued by the City that have gone unclaimed. This is our primary service focus.
  • State of Hawaii unclaimed property — the State of Hawaii Department of Budget and Finance maintains a database of unclaimed property reported by holders statewide under Hawaii Revised Statutes Chapter 523A. We check this database as an added service.

We research these public records, prepare claim documentation, and assist with claim submission on behalf of clients.

1.2 Free Government Alternatives

Both government offices provide free services for owners to search and claim directly:

  • Honolulu escheated checks: You may search and inquire directly with the City and County of Honolulu at honolulu.gov/bfs/escheated-checks/ at no cost.
  • State unclaimed property: You may search and file directly with the State of Hawaii at unclaimedproperty.ehawaii.gov/lilo/property-search.html at no cost.

You are not required to use our services to claim your funds. Our services are optional and offered for your convenience.
