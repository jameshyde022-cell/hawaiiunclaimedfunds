1. Services Description

1.1 Nature of Services

Hawaii Unclaimed Funds Recovery is a finder service that assists individuals and businesses in identifying and recovering unclaimed property held by the State of Hawaii pursuant to Hawaii Revised Statutes Chapter 523A. We research the state's unclaimed property database, prepare claim documentation, and submit claims on behalf of clients.

1.2 Free State Alternative

The State of Hawaii provides a free service for owners to search and claim unclaimed property directly through its online portal at dollars.ehawaii.gov. You are not required to use our services to claim your property. Our services are optional and offered for your convenience.

2.1 Compliance with HRS §523A-25

All finder agreements between the Company and clients are governed by and comply fully with Hawaii Revised Statutes §523A-25. This statute provides important consumer protections, including:

  • Cancellation rights during the period specified in your agreement
  • Prohibition of agreements that pay fees before property is recovered

2.2 Contingency Fee

Our fee for recovery services is 20% of the amount successfully recovered from the State of Hawaii. This fee is contingency-based and is due only after:

  • The State of Hawaii approves your claim; and
  • The State of Hawaii issues a disbursement notice confirming payment to you

If your claim is unsuccessful, you owe us nothing. We will never charge you an upfront fee, search fee, consultation fee, or any other fee prior to a successful recovery.

The State of Hawaii issues all payments directly to you, the rightful property owner. We do not receive, handle, hold, or in any way control or access your funds at any point. Our fee is a separate transaction you initiate after receiving your state-issued disbursement notice.
