Who We Are

Who We Are

Hawaii Unclaimed Funds Recovery is a private recovery assistance service helping Hawaii residents and small businesses navigate the process of recovering unclaimed property held by the State of Hawaii. We are not the State of Hawaii and are not affiliated with it.

What We Do

What We Do

🔍

Claim Review

We review your unclaimed property record and assess what documentation and steps are needed.

📋

Documentation & Paperwork

We help prepare, organize, and track the documents required by the State of Hawaii to process your claim.

🤝

Follow-Up & Support

We stay on top of the process, respond to State requests, and keep you informed at every step.

Important Limitations

What We Do Not Do

We are not the State of Hawaii. We are not affiliated with the State of Hawaii. We do not control claim approval or timelines. We do not receive client funds at any point. The State of Hawaii pays approved claims directly to the rightful owner.

Pricing

Our Fee

Our fee is 20% of the recovered amount. It is charged only after you receive payment directly from the State of Hawaii. If no funds are recovered, you owe nothing. You may also search and file directly with the State at no cost at dollars.ehawaii.gov.

Is This Right for You?

When Professional Help May Be Worth It

📋

Estate & Heir Claims

Claims involving a deceased relative require specific legal documentation. We handle these frequently.

🏢

Business Claims

Business or organizational claims have additional documentation requirements, including EIN and authorized signatory proof.

📍

Old or Missing Addresses

Claims with outdated addresses or missing documentation are common sources of delays and denials.

👥

Multiple Claimants

Claims involving multiple heirs or disputed ownership require careful coordination and documentation.

🔄

Denied or Delayed Claims

If your claim was denied or has stalled, we can review what happened and help with a corrected resubmission.

😌

Full-Service Management

Anyone who wants help managing the process from start to finish — so you don't have to chase the State on your own.

Client Intake

Leilani — Our Client Intake Assistant

When you call or submit a contact form, Leilani — our AI-powered intake assistant — may be the first to respond. Leilani is designed to gather the basic details of your situation and ensure our human team follows up promptly. All claim decisions, documentation guidance, and client communications are handled by our human staff.
