Our Mission

Help Hawaii Residents Recover What's Rightfully Theirs

The State of Hawaii is holding millions of dollars in unclaimed funds — money that legally belongs to Hawaii families and businesses. Much of it goes unclaimed simply because people don't know it exists, or find the claims process overwhelming.

Our mission is to bridge that gap. We use our knowledge of the state's unclaimed property system to help people who want professional assistance — while always being transparent that the state's search and claim process is free and available to everyone directly.

Our Core Belief

"Every Hawaii resident deserves to know if the state is holding money that belongs to them — and to receive honest, professional help recovering it if they want it, without being charged until they actually get their money."

Our Story

Why We Started This Business

It started with a family member. Like many people, we discovered that unclaimed funds can sit in a state database for years while the rightful owner remains unaware. When we went through the claims process ourselves, we realized how unnecessarily complicated and opaque it was — especially for estate claims involving a deceased relative.

We also noticed something troubling: the services that existed were either mainland companies with no local knowledge, or charged excessive upfront fees before doing anything. Hawaii families deserved better.

So we built the service we wished had existed — local, transparent, contingency-based, fully digital, and completely aligned with the interests of the people we serve.

Our Commitments

What We Promise Every Client

🔍

Radical Transparency

We tell you upfront that you can search and claim for free at dollars.ehawaii.gov. We show you the state database before asking for anything from you. No hidden information. No manufactured urgency.

💰

True Contingency

We will never charge you a single dollar until the State of Hawaii pays you. No exceptions. No service fees. No search fees. No cancellation fees. If you get nothing, we get nothing.

🤝

Honest Guidance

If your claim is simple enough to handle yourself, we'll tell you — even if it means we don't earn a fee. We'd rather send you to dollars.ehawaii.gov and keep your trust than take 20% of a claim you could have filed in 30 minutes.

🌺

Local Knowledge

We know Hawaii — the state system, the culture, the islands, and the community. We're not a mainland call center. When you call, you reach someone who lives and works in Hawaii.

🔒

Data Privacy

Your personal information is treated with the highest level of security. We use encrypted document transmission, secure storage, and we never sell or share your data with third parties.

📋

Full Compliance

Every agreement we sign complies fully with Hawaii Revised Statutes §523A-25 and all applicable Hawaii consumer protection laws. Your legal rights are protected at every step.
