Who We Are

Who We Are

Hawaii Unclaimed Funds Recovery is a private recovery assistance service focused on helping Hawaii residents and businesses identify and recover funds that may appear in public escheated-check and unclaimed-property records. We are not the State of Hawaii. We are not the City and County of Honolulu. We are not affiliated with any government agency.

Our Sources

What We Review

We review City and County of Honolulu escheated-check records and may also check the State of Hawaii Unclaimed Property database for additional funds. These are two separate public databases — a person or business may appear in one, both, or neither.

What We Do

What We Do

🔍

Trace & Contact

We attempt to trace rightful payees and explain where the record appears — in Honolulu escheated-check records, the State unclaimed property database, or both.

📋

Organize the Claim

We help organize claim information, prepare documentation, and assist with the recovery process when requested.

🤝

Follow-Up & Support

We stay on top of the process, respond to requests, and keep you informed at every step until the claim is resolved.

Important Limitations

What We Do Not Do

We are not the State of Hawaii. We are not the City and County of Honolulu. We are not affiliated with any government agency. We do not control claim approval. We do not guarantee recovery.

Pricing

Our Fee

Our fee is charged only if recovery is successful and only after the client receives funds. There are no upfront fees. You may also pursue claims directly through the appropriate government office at no cost — search Honolulu escheated-check records at Honolulu.gov or the State of Hawaii Unclaimed Property database at budget.hawaii.gov. Fee agreements may be subject to applicable law. This website is for informational purposes only and is not legal advice.

Is This Right for You?

When Professional Help May Be Worth It

Our service covers both Honolulu escheated checks and the State of Hawaii Unclaimed Property database. Here's when people find professional assistance most useful.

📋

Estate & Heir Claims

Claims involving a deceased relative require specific legal documentation. We handle these frequently for both Honolulu escheated checks and State unclaimed property.

🏢

Business Claims

Business or organizational claims have additional documentation requirements, including EIN and authorized signatory proof. We handle claims from both sources.

📍

Old or Missing Addresses

Claims with outdated addresses or missing documentation are common sources of delays and denials.

👥

Multiple Claimants

Claims involving multiple heirs or disputed ownership require careful coordination and documentation.

🔄

Denied or Delayed Claims

If your claim was denied or has stalled, we can review what happened and help with a corrected resubmission.

😌

Full-Service Management

Anyone who wants help managing the process from start to finish — so you don't have to chase anyone on your own.

Client Intake

Leilani — Our Client Intake Assistant

When you call or submit a contact form, Leilani — our AI-powered intake assistant — may be the first to respond. Leilani is designed to gather the basic details of your situation and ensure our human team follows up promptly. All claim decisions, documentation guidance, and client communications are handled by our human staff.
